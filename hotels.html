.Where to go ? Where to sleep ?

There is a lot to see on our beautiful planet Earth. We tried to select for you the most important to visit when you are here for a short time. To do that, you will need a place to stay, a place to be... On Earth, any hotel under 6 stars is not allowed to accept visitors from outer space. You will find here a place to stay at your convenience.

Paris, France

For a romantic place for two, discover Paris and our hotel which is the castle of Versailles itself ! You will enjoy our restaurants and spend the day shopping in our amazing shops : Channel, Gucci...

Please contact us for more information about reservation (17 years of queue for now)!

Tokyo, Japan

For solo trips and small budgets, discover the capsule hotels in Tokyo! You can go in and out at any time of the day and enjoy your trip as you want.

You will find a Baseball field at the top of our tower and you will have access to a super hot bath but you will have to go there naked of course.

California, USA

For a family vacation or a week with friends, you will enjoy our villa with private pool. There are 187 rooms with bathroom, 30 kitchens and a nightclub.

Explore the city in one of our 160 cars and eat our best burgers of California !

Manille, Philippines

For a beautiful honeymoon and if you are thirsty for adventure, discover the waters of the Pacific ! You can swim with sharks and discover the last corals.

Please contact us for more information and surprise about your honeymoon !
